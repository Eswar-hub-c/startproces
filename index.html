Common Queries

Answers to the questions we get asked most often.

How long does it take to build a website or app?

Timelines vary by scope. A standard business website takes 2–3 weeks, while a full web or mobile app can take 6–12 weeks. We define clear milestones in the agreement phase.

What is included in the ₹4999 starting package?

The starting price covers a single-page or simple multi-page website. All projects are custom scoped — we provide a detailed quote after an initial discovery call.

Do you offer post-launch maintenance and support?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, performance monitoring, and growth support plans to ensure your product evolves with your business needs.

Can you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. We serve clients globally and operate across multiple time zones. Communication is primarily handled via WhatsApp, email, and video calls.

Do you provide branding and design-only services?

Yes. Our Design & Creative team can independently handle logo design, brand identity, UI/UX design, and video editing without a development engagement.
